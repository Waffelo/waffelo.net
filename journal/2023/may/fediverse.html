Drgnz Fediverse

2nd May 2023

Few days ago me and Jakub started selfohosting our own Akkoma instance on the Drgnz.club network. Akkoma is a nice and lightweight fediverse server software. We are intending this instance to be used for general purpose, with no specific theme. Well, I could say that it is more leaning on the technological, floof and outdoor themes.

Some of our services have been moved to the drgnz.club domain. We moved the peertube, matrix, invidious and libreddit. We are planning to move git aswell.
